## Scoring Patterns and Insights

### Perfect Score (100) Indicators
- $100K+ funding
- 6-12 month timeline
- Pre-RFP/unsolicited stage
- Target state geography
- Example: MD-WF-2025-001, PA-AI-2025-008

### High Score (85-95) Characteristics
- Strong funding
- Good timeline or pre-RFP stage (compensates for other factors)
- Target geography
- Actionable within resources
- Examples: VA-ED-2025-012, MD-AI-2025-007, FED-WF-2025-021

### Moderate Score (70-84) Characteristics
- Good on some dimensions, challenges on others
- May require partnerships
- Competitive but winnable
- Worth pursuing with strategy
- Examples: DE-WF-2025-003, DC-ED-2025-006

### Lower Score (50-69) Characteristics
- Smaller funding OR tight timeline OR poor fit
- May be worth monitoring but not immediate pursuit
- Better opportunities likely exist
- Example: VA-WF-2025-018

### Monitor Only (<50) Indicators
- Multiple barriers (funding, timeline, fit, geography)
- Not strategic priority
- Archive but don't pursue
- Example: PA-ED-2025-019

### Out-of-Region Can Still Score High
- If opportunity is exceptional (funding, timing, fit)
- Geography penalty still applies but other factors compensate
- May warrant strategic geographic expansion discussion
- Example: NC-WF-2025-014
