🎓 How It Works

Interactive Web Search (Not Automated)

Step 1: You ask Claude: "Search for funding opportunities in Texas"

Step 2: Claude uses WebSearch tool to query Grants.gov, state sites, agency pages

Step 3: Claude analyzes results, filters by YOUR config, scores opportunities

Step 4: Claude presents findings with intelligence briefs and recommendations

This all happens in real-time during your conversation - no automation or actors!

1. Pre-RFP Signal Detection

Finds opportunities before they become competitive RFPs:

  • Legislative bills with appropriation language
  • Budget proposals with new funding
  • Agency strategic plans (unfunded priorities)
  • Listening sessions and stakeholder engagement
  • Expiring appropriations

2. Intelligent Scoring (0-100)

Every opportunity is scored based on YOUR priorities:

Factor Points What It Means
Funding Size 40 Based on your funding preferences
Timeline 20 6-12 months = optimal positioning
Competition 20 Unsolicited = highest score
Geography 20 Your target states = max points

* Weights are customizable in your configuration

3. Smart Routing Tags

  • white-paper-ready (80+): Write thought leadership, position early
  • proposal-ready (70+): Active RFP, develop proposal
  • nurture-needed (50-69): Build relationships
  • monitor-only (<50): Archive for reference

4. Custom Intelligence Reports

  • Individual opportunity briefs
  • Weekly digests
  • Monthly trend analysis
  • High-priority alerts (your thresholds)

⚙️ Easy Setup (10-15 Minutes)

1 Download the skill package

2 Open CONFIG-TEMPLATE.md and fill in:

  • Your business profile and services
  • Your target states/regions
  • Your industry focus areas
  • Your custom keywords
  • Your funding thresholds
  • Your scoring preferences

3 Save as CONFIG.md in the skill folder

4 ZIP the folder (include your CONFIG.md)

5 Upload to Claude → Settings → Capabilities → Skills

6 Test it:

"Search for funding opportunities in my target regions"

💡 Configuration Examples

Example 1: California Training Provider

primary_states: [California] primary_keywords: [workforce training, technical skills, job placement] minimum_funding: $25,000 sweet_spot_range: $50,000 - $250,000

Example 2: Multi-State Economic Development Consultant

primary_states: [North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Virginia] regions: [Appalachian region] primary_keywords: [economic development, entrepreneurship, regional planning] minimum_funding: $100,000 sweet_spot_range: $250,000 - $1,000,000

Example 3: Manufacturing Industry Association

primary_states: [Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, Michigan] primary_keywords: [manufacturing, Industry 4.0, AI workforce, automation] minimum_funding: $500,000 sweet_spot_range: $1,000,000 - $5,000,000

Example 4: Texas Nonprofit
