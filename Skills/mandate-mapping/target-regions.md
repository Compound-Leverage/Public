## Key Contact Types to Identify

For each high-scoring opportunity, attempt to identify:

1. **Program Officer/Manager:** Direct contact for questions
2. **Agency Leadership:** Commissioner, Secretary, Director level
3. **Workforce Board Executive Director:** Strategic relationship
4. **Legislative Staff:** Committee staff for relevant committees
5. **Governor's Policy Staff:** Workforce/economic development advisors
6. **Regional/Local Administrators:** One-stop operators, local board staff
7. **Industry Partnership Leads:** Sector-specific initiatives
8. **Past Awardees:** Learn from successful applicants
