## Exclusion Criteria (Do Not Include)

### Out of Scope Programs
- Pure K-12 education (no workforce component)
- Higher education scholarships only
- Medical research grants
- Basic scientific research (no training component)
- Construction/infrastructure only (no workforce development)
- Equipment purchases only
- Facilities/capital projects only
- Emergency relief/disaster recovery (unless includes workforce component)
- Pure childcare/social services
- Housing programs
- Transportation infrastructure (unless workforce training included)

### Employment vs. Training
**EXCLUDE:**
- Job postings
- Employment opportunities
- Staff positions
- Contractor positions
- Consultant RFPs for services (not training)
- Vendor opportunities

**INCLUDE:**
- Programs that create jobs AND provide training
- Employment services with skill development
- Transitional jobs with training component

### Award Announcements (Already Closed)
- Grant awarded to
- [Organization] receives funding
- Congratulations to
- Award recipient
- Grant winner
- Awarded contract
- Past performance
- Success story (unless announcing new round)

### International/Out of Region
- International development
- Foreign aid
- Global programs (unless US component)
- States outside target region (unless federal program)
- Tribal-only programs (unless target states)

### Wrong Industry/Sector
- Agricultural extension only
- Military training (unless transferable skills)
- Law enforcement training only
- Firefighter training only
- Teacher professional development only
- Medical continuing education
- Legal profession CLE

### Administrative/Overhead
- Technical assistance only
- Evaluation contracts
- Research contracts
- Audit services
- Consulting services (not training delivery)
- Data system procurement
- Software licenses

## Boolean Search Strings

### Grants.gov Search
```
("workforce development" OR "workforce training" OR "skills training")
AND ("artificial intelligence" OR "AI" OR "automation" OR "digital transformation")
AND (Maryland OR Virginia OR Pennsylvania OR Delaware OR "District of Columbia" OR "Washington DC")
```

### Legislative Search (Example for Maryland)
```
(workforce OR training OR "economic development")
AND (AI OR "artificial intelligence" OR automation OR digital)
AND (appropriation OR funding OR grant OR program)
AND (bill OR legislation OR budget)
```

### Google Search (News/Press Releases)
```
site:maryland.gov OR site:virginia.gov OR site:pa.gov OR site:delaware.gov
("workforce development" OR "economic development")
AND (funding OR grant OR initiative OR program)
AND (AI OR automation OR technology)
-jobs -careers -employment (to exclude job postings)
```

### Agency Website Search
```
("funding opportunity" OR "grant program" OR RFP OR RFA OR NOFO)
AND (workforce OR training OR "economic development")
AND (2025 OR 2026)
-archived -closed -past
```

## Quality Filters

### Minimum Requirements to Capture
- Funding amount: At least $10,000
- OR Strategic importance (even if funding unclear)
- OR Pre-RFP signal with high potential
- Geographic nexus to target states
- Workforce or economic development connection

### Auto-Exclude
- Funding under $5,000 (unless strategic)
- Deadlines already passed
- Award announcements (already closed)
- Job postings
- Pure goods/services procurement
- Completely out of region

## Alert Triggers (Immediate Notification)

### High-Priority Alerts
- Funding over $1 million
- Unsolicited opportunity in target state
- Pre-RFP signal with near-term funding
- Multi-year program announcement
- State/federal partnership opportunity
- "Anticipated release" notice for upcoming RFP

### Medium-Priority Alerts
- Funding $250K-$1M
- Pre-proposal conference announced
- Concept paper deadline
- Legislative hearing on relevant topic
- New agency division/office created
- Strategic plan released with funding needs

## False Positive Mitigation

### Common Mismatches to Filter
1. **Job Training vs. Job Posting:** Ensure it's a program/grant, not a job
2. **Past vs. Future:** Verify it's an active or upcoming opportunity
3. **Goods vs. Services:** Confirm it's not just buying equipment/software
4. **Wrong Workforce:** Filter out medical CE, teacher PD, law enforcement unless transferable
5. **Geographic Mismatch:** Verify state eligibility carefully

### Validation Questions
- Is this actually a funding opportunity or just news about a program?
- Is the deadline still in the future?
- Can organizations in our target states apply?
- Does it have a workforce development or economic development component?
- Is the funding amount sufficient to pursue (over minimum threshold)?
